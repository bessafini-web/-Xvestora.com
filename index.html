Common Questions

What Investors
Ask Before Starting

Do you list available properties?

No. We do not maintain a public catalog. We source land specifically for each investor brief. Every opportunity is activated after receiving your criteria.

Are you a licensed real estate agency?

Xvistora operates as an advisory and sourcing network, not as a traditional brokerage. We work with licensed notaries and legal experts for all transactional and compliance steps.

Can I invest remotely from abroad?

Yes. Most of our investors operate from Europe or the Gulf. We execute on the ground in Morocco and report to you throughout. Notarial processes can be handled remotely by power of attorney.

What is the minimum investment size?

We work with serious investors across a range of budgets. We recommend starting with a clear brief that includes your budget range — we will tell you directly what is realistic in your target market.

How is Xvistora compensated?

We operate on a success-based advisory fee structure agreed with each investor before work begins. There are no upfront fees for the initial brief and sourcing phase.

How long does a typical transaction take?

From brief to shortlist: 48h to 7 days. From shortlist to signed deal: typically 4 to 12 weeks, depending on due diligence complexity and administrative timelines.
